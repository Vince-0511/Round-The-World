About Us

Round The World (RTW) is a travel agency built on the belief that travel should be accessible, meaningful, and inspiring for everyone. We are dedicated to creating unforgettable journeys that connect people to cultures, destinations, and experiences across the globe.

Founded with a passion for exploration and discovery, RTW was created to simplify travel planning and remove the stress from organising trips. We provide carefully curated travel packages that combine comfort, affordability, safety, and authentic local experiences, ensuring every journey is both enjoyable and enriching.

Our services are designed to suit all types of travellers — solo explorers, families, couples, groups, and corporate travellers. From cultural heritage tours and adventure travel to luxury escapes and leisure holidays, every RTW experience is thoughtfully planned to meet diverse travel needs and expectations.

At RTW, we work with trusted partners, experienced guides, and reliable service providers to ensure high-quality service and seamless travel experiences. Our commitment to excellence, customer care, and personalised service sets us apart as a travel agency that truly values every traveller’s journey.

Round The World is more than a travel agency — it is a gateway to the world. Round The World — Connecting You to the World, One Journey at a Time.

Our Services

Travel Package Planning

We design customised travel packages tailored to different travel styles, budgets, and preferences, ensuring every journey is well-planned and stress-free.

Accommodation & Transport Booking

We arrange reliable accommodation and transportation services, including hotels, guesthouses, flights, and ground transport for a smooth travel experience.

Guided Tours & Local Experiences

We provide guided tours and authentic local experiences led by trusted local guides, allowing travellers to explore destinations safely and meaningfully.

Travel Consultation & Planning

Our consultants offer professional travel advice, itinerary planning, and destination guidance to help travellers make informed travel decisions.

Group & Corporate Travel Management

We manage group tours, educational trips, corporate travel, and incentive tours with organised scheduling and full logistical support.

Travel Support & Assistance

We provide ongoing customer support before, during, and after the journey, ensuring safety, comfort, and peace of mind throughout the trip.
